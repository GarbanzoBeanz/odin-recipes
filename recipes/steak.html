Steak Recipe

This is a recipe to the best steak that I have tried.

The recipe is incredibly simple, with minimal kitchen equipment needed.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Beef
  • Garlic Power
  • Onions
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Dry brine your Beef (add salt and let it sit)
  2. Use paper towels to pat the Beef until fully dry
  3. Season your beef with pepper and garlic powder
  4. Get your pan piping hot
  5. Sear your Beef on all sides
  6. Add butter and baste
  7. Remove steak and let it rest
  8. Reduce heat and add onions and brown sugar
  9. Cook until caramelized (not burnt!)
  10. Top the beef with the onions and leftover pan sauce
  11. Enjoy your Steak
